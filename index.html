California Facts

Capital

Sacramento is the capital of California.

Sacramento is the capital of California. The city is located at the confluence of the Sacramento River and the American River in Northern California's Sacramento Valley. Sacramento is the sixth-largest city in California and the ninth largest capital in the United States.
