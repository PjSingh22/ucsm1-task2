San Francisco

San Francisco, officially the City and County of San Francisco, is a commercial, financial, and cultural center in Northern California.

San Francisco was incorporated on December 16, 1848. The town was originally called Yerba Buena before the discovery of gold.

Fun Facts

  • The population of San Francisco is 808,437 (2022)
  • San Francisco is located in the San Francisco Bay Area.
  • San Francisco is classified as a urban city with a high population density and built environment.
  • The average income in San Francisco is 65k (2022)
