San Diego

San Diego is a city on the Pacific coast of California known for its beaches, parks and warm climate.

San Diego was incorporated on March 27, 1848. The town was originally called Yerba Buena before the discovery of gold.

Fun Facts

  • The population of San Diego is 1.3 million (2022)
  • San Diego is located in Southen California.
  • San Diego is classified as a urban city with a high population density and built environment.
  • The average income in San Diego is 45k (2022)
