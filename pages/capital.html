Sacramento

Sacramento is the capital of California. The city is located at the confluence of the Sacramento River and the American River in Northern California's Sacramento Valley. Sacramento is the sixth-largest city in California and the ninth largest capital in the United States.

Sacramento was incorporated on February 27, 1850. It is the oldest incorporated city in California.

Fun Facts

  • Sacramento is the sunniest place on the planet during the month of July.
  • The city is home to the California State Railroad Museum, which is the largest museum of its kind in North America.
  • Sacramento is the Farm-to-Fork Capital of America.
  • The city is home to the oldest newspaper in California, the Sacramento Bee.
